We began as KJK Designs, a website design and development company as an enterprise in June 2014. In 2015, we became incorporated as a limited liability company but began full operations as such in January 2014. Since then, KJK Africa has served over 100 clients in different industries. We’ve built technology and designed systems that have successfully reduced production costs, increased productivity, and revenue.
For the foreseeable future, we are focused on helping businesses improve production process, strengthen customer relationship, and scale profit.
