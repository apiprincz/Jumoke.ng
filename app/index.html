About me

I am passionate about building tech solutions that people will use to improve their everyday lives.

I am a Software Product Developer, and the CEO of KJK Africa, one of Nigeria’s leading software design and Development Company based in Lagos. At KJK, I lead a team of tech experts to build technology products and deliver services that help businesses optimize production and service processes, deepen customer relationship, and grow revenue.

I have also developed the expertise and experience to help non-tech businesses venture into the tech space with ready to market products and services.
