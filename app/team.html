Adebola Kupoluyi's Profile

Adebola is a seasoned professional with over nine (9) years of valuable experience garnered in diverse, challenging roles. She possesses broad management experience overseeing key functions of Finance Operations, Quality Assurance, Health & Safety, and Network Operations. As Senior Manager, Finance Operations at American Towers, she currently manages 30+ team members and ensures there are adequate controls to manage the disbursement of the yearly operational maintenance budget.
She gained four (4) years consulting experience in KPMG with focus in Enterprise Risk Management (ERM), internal audit planning and execution, budget forecasting monitoring and control, Sarbanes Oxley (SOX) reviews, process reengineering, revenue assurance, fraud investigations and project management from diverse sectors such as Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), oil & gas, telecommunications, banking, and health sectors.
Burdened by the number of street kids, she started Mantle of Mordecai (MOM) foundation, a registered non-profit organisation with the mission to reduce the population of out-of-school children in Nigeria and by extension Africa. She also champions the Culture Preservation Initiative with the mandate of ensuring that the culture of her home country Nigeria is preserved all over the world. As part of impacting her world, she is a volunteer mentor of The Tony Elumelu Foundation tasked with empowering young entrepreneurs in Africa.

Onyeka Akumah's Profile

Onyeka Akumah is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Farmcrowdy – a startup he launched in November 2016 and is currently referred to as Nigeria's First Digital Agriculture platform.
Impacting on the lives of over 11,000 small-scale farmers already since inception in November 2016, Farmcrowdy has created and is leading a new category of online platforms that are focused on increasing food production. The company is working to impact positively on the lives of 50,000 small scale farmers in Nigeria in the next five years and engage 2 million Nigerian youths to participate in Agriculture through technology.
Before Farmcrowdy, Onyeka was the Chief Commercial Officer of Travel beta – one of Nigeria's leading online travel agencies. He has also worked with Konga as Vice President; Marketing, Jumia as Director of Marketing & Partnerships, Wakanow as Online Marketing Manager, E-Marketing Coordinator for Deloitte for West, East, and Central Africa, and Webmaster for British Council in Nigeria.
In addition to being the CEO of Farmcrowdy, Onyeka currently sits on the board of leading tech startups in New York and Lagos and continuously seek ways to impact on Nigerian youths through media and technology.

Olayemi Keri Profile

Yemi Keri is one of the foremost women in Technology in Nigeria. She is the Chief Executive Officer of Heckerbella Limited, a Technology Business Transformation company, and also a Partner at Holmen Consulting, an Information Technology Consulting firm.
She has over 21 years' experience in the Information and Communications Technology sector at top management levels in both the public and private sectors.
Yemi Keri held the post of Director and Regional Manager, West Africa for SAP Africa and was the pioneer Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of the Edo State Directorate of Information Communications & Technology Agency. She is committed to creating an enabling environment for women in tech and business.
Yemi Keri is the co-founder of Rising Tide Africa, a women's movement initiated with a vision to increase women's participation in angel investing as an asset class and promote education, cross-border investing, and investor-mentoring across Africa.
Yemi Keri is a member of the Lagos Angel Network and serves as a mentor to various digitally and technology-enabled startups and established businesses like KJK Africa.

Charles Ojei Profile

Charles Ojei is a business development expert focused on creating innovative business models to help drive economic and social impact in emerging markets. He has extensive experience in consumer goods, agriculture, and energy sectors and has worked for several multinationals.
Since 2016, Charles Ojei has been at the helm of HYBR - a Pan African innovation firm and growth platform for impact scaleups. HYBR works with entrepreneurs, multinational corporations, governments, non-profits, and investors to scale solutions, leveraging the intersection of technology, business, and humanities to solve big problems and drive sustainable impact.
Before founding HYBR, Charles held several positions at different multinational organisations. Notably, He was Director of Social Innovation & Economic Development for Samsung West Africa. Service Sales Leader Africa for General Electric Healthcare.
In most of his roles, he won recognition for outstanding service and achievement. A firm in the Pan-African vision, one of Charles favourite mantra, is – we rise by lifting others. He is committed to helping African businesses thrive and is excited about the economic potential of Africa.

Ismail Damcida Profile

Ismail Damcida is an international trade consultant with over a decade of experience in international trade and export business development. He has a wealth of knowledge and experience of worldwide markets, which is complemented by his impressive local and international network of partners and associates.
Throughout his career, Ismail Damcida has been involved in international business; developing and implementing strategies, building teams and distribution networks to open new markets.
Damcida is also a passionate environmentalist and humanitarian dedicated to promoting sustainable development to unlock real value that improves the lives of people and their environment.
Damcida holds a Bsc in IRPM, an American Institute of management, diploma in human resource management and also a certificate in international trade documentation. Damcida serves as a Director on numerous boards and also the country representative of HEIS global pharmaceutical & nutraceutical packaging Spain and Aiscan Spain, Europe's largest producer of electric conduit.
